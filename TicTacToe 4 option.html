How to Play

Game Board

The game is played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns placing their mark (X or O) in empty squares.

Win Condition

The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game.

Draw Condition

If all 9 squares are filled and no player has 3 in a row, the game ends in a draw.
